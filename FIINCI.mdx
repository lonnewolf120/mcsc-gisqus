# MIST Cyber Security Club Hosts Financial Institute & CIIs Cyber Drill 2023

The **Military Institute of Science and Technology (MIST) Cyber Security Club** proudly organized the **Financial Institute & Critical Information Infrastructures (CIIs) Cyber Drill 2023**. This event brought together professionals from various financial institutions and CIIs to showcase their cybersecurity expertise and readiness.

Under the banner of MIST, this competition served as a crucial platform to address emerging cyber threats and promote cybersecurity best practices. The event not only highlighted the importance of cybersecurity preparedness but also allowed institutions to exchange valuable insights.
