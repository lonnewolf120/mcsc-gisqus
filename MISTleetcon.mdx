# MIST LeetCon 2023: HackMeIfYouCan - Bangladesh's First International Cybersecurity Conference

MIST LeetCon 2023: HackMeIfYouCan - the First Ever International Cybersecurity Conference in Bangladesh - was successfully hosted by MIST and co-organized by MIST Cyber Security Club (MCSC) and MIST Computer Club (MCC) on 20 May 2023. The mega event was arranged in partnership with the ICT Division of Bangladesh (ICTD), Bangladesh Computer Council (BCC), and Digital Security Agency (DSA) and in sponsorship with VMware, F5, and Trust Bank Ltd. The Hon’ble Minister of State for Information and Communication Division of Bangladesh Mr. Zunaid Ahmed Palak was the chief guest. Alongside the great initiative to promote cyber awareness, the enthusiasm and ardent participation of the attendees undoubtedly played a major part in making the event a huge success.

***

## Inaugural Ceremony

MIST LeetCon 2023 featured a Capture the Flag (CTF) contest titled “HackMeIfYouCan” with prize money worth a total of 500k BDT and several unmissable workshops from renowned national and international cybersecurity experts!

The lively inaugural ceremony of the event was presided over by the Dean of the Faculty of Electrical and Computer Engineering (FECE) and Head of the Department of Computer Science and Engineering of MIST, Brigadier General Md Mahfuzul Karim Majumder, ndc, psc, te.

With about 100 teams from universities and corporations such as Bangladesh Army, Bangladesh Police, and Dhaka Stock Exchange Ltd. competing in the CTF competition, and hundreds of attendees registering for the workshop, MIST LeetCon 2023 set out on a smooth journey to the wide realm of cybersecurity.

***

## Capture the Flag (CTF) Competition

“HackMeIfYouCan” - the CTF competition of MIST LeetCon 2023, was one of the most popular events. Qualifying in the online preliminary round held on 6 May 2023, hundreds of teams struggled to solve challenging tasks of finding flags from vulnerable systems. Eight teams from MIST qualified and competed in the onsite round.

After a fierce 6-hour-long cyber battle, HackMeIfYouCan finally concluded, with team “Red Rebels” from Cyber Security Club, Daffodil International University emerging as the champion. Team “Silent Killer” and Team “Federal Bonk Investigations” were announced as the First Runner Up and Second Runner Up, respectively.

## Workshops

Besides the thrilling CTF competition, five workshops were held in the Multipurpose Hall of MIST, featuring interactive Q\&A sessions with cybersecurity experts. These workshops provided invaluable insights and hands-on experiences for participants.

## Closing Ceremony

The closing ceremony of MIST LeetCon 2023 started after 5 PM with the respected Acting Commandant of MIST, Brigadier General Mohammad Wahidul Islam, sup, ndc, psc, extending his gratitude to the attendees. Remarkable speeches were given by the Executive Director of Bangladesh Computer Council (BCC), Ranajit Kumar, Director General of Digital Security Agency (DSA), Nahid Sultana Mallik, and Brigadier General Md Mahfuzul Karim Majumder (HOD, CSE), lauding the great initiative and appreciating the CTF contestants.

With the faculty members and volunteers working tirelessly, MIST Leetcon 2023: HackMeIfYouCan marked a significant milestone in the cybersecurity journey of Bangladesh. With greater support and determination, MIST is set to host more conferences like this in the future, with hope for more success in the years to come.
