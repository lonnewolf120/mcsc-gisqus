# Congratulations to MIST\_Hamsters and CR4CK\_P3G45U5 Teams for BUET Capture the Flag Competition 2023

We announce our most sincere congratulations to the **MIST\_Hamsters** and **CR4CK\_P3G45U5** teams for their remarkable performance in the **BUET Inter-University Capture the Flag** competition held on **21st July 2023**.

In the Preliminary stage held on **16 July 2023**, 30 teams out of more than 120 teams from universities and institutions all over the country qualified for the Final round, with MIST\_Hamsters and CR4CK\_P3G45U5 being two of the qualified teams! We are proud to share that both teams have secured the **15th** and **18th** positions respectively in the Final round of this fiercely competitive event.

Throughout the competition, these teams have continuously displayed extraordinary skill, steadfast determination, and impressive problem-solving abilities, as well as showcasing their in-depth understanding of cybersecurity and ethical hacking. Their remarkable performance demonstrates their dedication and capacity to tackle complicated problems with resilience.
