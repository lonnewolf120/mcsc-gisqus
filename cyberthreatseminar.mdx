# Measures and Preparedness for Emerging Cyber Threats Seminar 2023

The **"Measures and Preparedness for Emerging Cyber Threats"** seminar, held on **October 1, 2023**, was a highly anticipated and well-attended event organized by the esteemed **Department of Computer Science and Engineering** at the **Military Institute of Science and Technology (MIST)**, in collaboration with the **Armed Forces Division (AFD)** of Bangladesh.

<img height="695" width="1044" alt="[object Object]" src="https://scontent.fdac7-1.fna.fbcdn.net/v/t39.30808-6/385078814_279078528290588_5978178374366673704_n.jpg?_nc_cat=100&ccb=1-7&_nc_sid=127cfc&_nc_eui2=AeEovhLFASc6YooGMocxx4LbAkCEI_Z4RugCQIQj9nhG6P6kNLf_dc-Hg9kZ5KrESaOVVJzX2KLa_ZiP8yPceeBD&_nc_ohc=pLy5wxepkOYQ7kNvgH9198t&_nc_ht=scontent.fdac7-1.fna&_nc_gid=AU5RoC9dMo_wMssr-wqhObF&oh=00_AYA1MS4V673JQ94RnYsR5yZOrjGlKafje3RfUCqikF0y4w&oe=66E11BEA" />

The seminar showcased a wide range of activities, including engaging keynote addresses, thought-provoking panel discussions, and valuable insights into both current and anticipated cyber threats.

### Distinguished Guests

Distinguished guests in attendance included:

* **Zunaid Ahmed Palak, MP**, Hon'ble State Minister of Information and Communication Technology (ICT Division), Bangladesh, as Chief Guest.
* **Lt Gen Waker-Uz-Zaman, SGp, psc**, Principal Staff Officer (PSO) of the Armed Forces Division as Special Guest.
* **Maj Gen Mohammad Saidul Islam, rcds, ndc, psc**, Commandant of MIST as Chairman of the seminar.

The seminar attracted an impressive number of participants from various sectors, including the Armed Forces, prestigious universities, and esteemed institutions, including MIST itself.

### Topics Covered

The topics covered during the event were diverse and comprehensive, encompassing crucial aspects such as:

* Effective measures and preparedness against cyber threats
* Public awareness and regulatory frameworks
* The vital concept of cyber resilience
