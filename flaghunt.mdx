# Flaghunt 2023: A Successful Cybersecurity Event at MIST

The MIST CSE Department was proud to host **Flaghunt 2023**, an annual cybersecurity competition that challenges the skills of cybersecurity enthusiasts, organized by **CTF Community Bangladesh**. This successful event was largely attributed to the dedicated members of the **MIST Cyber Security Club**, who volunteered their expertise and effort.

Their commitment ensured a competitive and enriching atmosphere, as well as a flawless conclusion to the event. We express our sincere gratitude to these volunteers for their vital role in ensuring the success of **Flaghunt 2023**, which demonstrated the MIST Cyber Security Club member's exceptional technical expertise and teamwork.
