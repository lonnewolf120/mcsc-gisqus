To celebrate the arrival of newcomers, MIST is introducing their most enhanced and advanced IT section — **Cyber Security Club**. The club brings an exciting opportunity to all cyber enthusiasts and serves as a reliable platform to hone their skills and prepare themselves as challengers in the cyber wars.

**Cyber Security Club** offers:

* CTF preparations
* Penetration testing
* Open source intelligence
* Reconnaissance
* Digital Forensics
* Red teaming, blue teaming
* ...and many more!

It's time to build a community with many others who share your passion. The **MIST Cyber Security Club** is open for 𝐚𝐥𝐥 𝐝𝐞𝐩𝐚𝐫𝐭𝐦𝐞𝐧𝐭𝐬. So, brace yourself and join the cyber warrior community, because—
